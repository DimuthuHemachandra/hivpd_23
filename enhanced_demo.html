Enhanced Recruitment Statistics

Interactive visualization with expandable details for HIV, PD, and Control groups

How to Use This Interactive Dashboard

  • Click on summary cards at the top to expand detailed breakdowns for each diagnosis group
  • Click on chart segments in the Diagnosis Distribution chart to see group-specific details
  • Hover over timeline points to see monthly recruitment details including:
    • New recruits by diagnosis group (HIV, PD, Controls)
    • Cognitive tests and scans completed that month
    • Cumulative totals
  • View statistical differences between groups including chi-square and p-values
  • Expandable sections show:
    • Gender ratios for each diagnosis group
    • Completion status (cognitive testing & scans)
    • Upcoming visits breakdown
  • Click the X button in expanded sections to close them
